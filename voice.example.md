# Voice

Copy this file to `voice.md` and fill it with your company's real writing. Voice lives
in a file because it is the thing you will edit most, and it belongs in version
control next to the code.

Two rules that matter:

**Write what TO do.** Describe how you write, concretely. A rule phrased "never use X"
produces prose visibly braced against X, which reads worse than the thing it banned.

**Examples beat adjectives.** "Plainspoken and direct" carries almost no information.
Three paragraphs of your actual writing carry all of it. Paste in work you are proud
of, below.

## How we write

(describe it: sentence length, warmth, how technical you get, how you open and close)
