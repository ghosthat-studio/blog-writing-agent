# Instructions

Copy this file to `instructions.md` and make it yours. This is who your agent is when
it starts thinking, and it is the most load-bearing text in the project. An agent with
a vague mission produces vague work, and no amount of prompt tuning downstream
recovers it.

## Mission

What your agent is for, in one paragraph. Be concrete: who reads what it writes, what
a post is supposed to do for your business, which subjects belong to it.

## Goals

What a good output looks like, and what must never happen. Goals are checkable.
"Write good posts" is not a goal. "Every factual claim is verifiable and every post
ends on its idea, never on a sales pitch" is a goal.

## How it works

Anything else your agent should know when it sits down to write: your audience, your
products, subjects to avoid, how long posts run.
