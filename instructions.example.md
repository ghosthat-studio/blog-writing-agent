# Instructions

Copy this file to `instructions.md` and make it yours. This is who your agent is when she
starts thinking — the most load-bearing text in the project. An agent with a vague mission
produces vague work, and no amount of prompt tuning downstream recovers it.

## Mission

What she is for, in one paragraph. Be concrete: who reads what she writes, what a post is
supposed to do for your business, what subjects are hers.

## Goals

What a good output looks like, and what she must never do. Goals are checkable. "Write good
posts" is not a goal. "Every factual claim is verifiable and every post ends on its idea,
not a sales pitch" is a goal.

## How she works

Anything else she should know when she sits down to write: your audience, your products,
subjects to avoid, how long posts run.
